Paneer Cheese Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Paneer
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

How to make Paneer Pizza

  1. Take 1 teaspoon instant yeast (3 grams) and ½ teaspoon sugar in a mixing bowl or a tray.
  2. Add 1 cup lukewarm water.
  3. Mix very well so that the yeast granules dissolve in water.
  4. Next, add 3 cups whole wheat flour, 3 tablespoons olive oil and 1 teaspoon salt.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon lemon juice. If you do not have lemon juice, then add 1 tablespoon buttermilk or fresh curd (yogurt).
  6. Begin to knead the dough.
  7. Knead to a smooth and soft dough. Add more lukewarm water if required while kneading. Knead for at least 8 to 10 minutes.
  8. Place the dough in a bowl. Brush or spread some olive oil all over the dough.
  9. Place a cotton kitchen towel covering the dough or pan. Allow to leaven for 45 minutes to 2 hours or till the dough increases in volume and doubles up. Timing will vary with the temperature conditions in your city.
  10. After the dough has leavened well, it will increase in volume and double or triple up.
  11. Prepare the toppings while the dough is leavening. Take 1 cup onion cubes, 1 cup bell pepper cubes (capsicum) and ½ cup roughly chopped spinach (about 12 to 15 small to medium size spinach leaves) in a mixing bowl.
  12. Add 100 grams paneer cubes.
  13. Add the following ground spices:
    • ¼ teaspoon red chili powder or cayenne pepper
    • ¼ teaspoon cumin powder
    • ¼ teaspoon garam masala powder
    • 1 pinch salt
  14. Gently mix and toss the paneer and vegetables with the curd, spice powders and salt.Cover and keep aside to marinate for 20 to 30 minutes or more. If keeping for more than 30 minutes, then refrigerate.
  15. Take the well leaved dough and gently knock back. Knead the dough once again with light hands. Make a log shape from the dough and slice in 4 to 5 equal parts. For thin crust pizzas, cut the dough in 6 to 7 parts.
  16. Spread some olive oil on a 9 to 10 inch baking tray. Sprinkle some semolina (rava/sooji) evenly all over the tray. You can even use flour or cornmeal for sprinkling.Also, preheat the oven to its maximum temperature for 15 to 20 minutes before baking.
  17. Take a pizza dough part, sprinkle some flour on it and begin to roll it using a rolling pin.
  18. Roll to a medium thick base having a thickness of about ¼ to ⅓ inches. You can even make thin crust pizzas.
  19. Carefully lift and place the pizza base in the greased baking pan or tray.
  20. With a knife or fork, poke the rolled pizza base all over.
  21. Spread 1.5 to 2 tablespoons homemade pizza sauce on the dough.
  22. reak fresh mozzarella cheese into pieces and place on the sauce. Add mozzarella cheese depending on the size of the pizza.Use pizza cheese if you don’t have mozzarella cheese.
  23. Arrange the marinated vegetables and paneer. Again, do not overcrowd with the toppings.
  24. Keep the pizza pan in the center rack using both the top and bottom heating elements in the preheated oven.
  25. Bake Paneer Pizza for 15 to 20 minutes or till the cheese melts and the pizza crust is crisp and golden.
  26. Remove the pizza on a plate, tray or board. Sprinkle some red chili flakes and oregano on the pizza. You can also top the pizza with some torn fresh basil leaves.
